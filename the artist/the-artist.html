MAROUANE​ BAAZI​Z

Marouane Baaziz is a young 16-year-old artist who discovered his passion in the third art, precisely photography. His first debut is the year 2019, and this year 2023 he created his own gallery "Art-ftswira", where he shares his art which he captures with an artistic look in an image that has neither meaning nor unique teaching , where opinions don't matter in agreement on a single topic. That's why he decided to work on this idea as his new beginning and also to know people's opinions and views about his work.
